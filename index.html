ABOUT

<hack-IT-on/> is the official Tech club of Bengal Institute of Technology.It provides a forum and workspace in which to share ideas about and collaborate on projects related to Android App Development & Web Development. Students who participate in the club will find opportunities to learn programming languages, develop and test their code, explore innovative uses, experiment with new things, and investigate how applications function. Our goal is to offer a social and educational environment in which would-be developers, no matter their level of expertise, can improve their skills and gain confidence with all things digital.
<hack-IT-on/> started out as a dorm room idea back in 2017 by a few unorthodox driven students of Bengal Institute of Technology(Techno Group) who wanted to move away from the realm of the normal academic routine and organise some interesting classes to help expand the thinking of students, enhance their exposures and show that you don't always need a lifetime to make something extraordinary. Sometimes, you can do it in few hours too. Beating it's own stats with every passing year, <hack-IT-on/> has undoubtedly grown exponentially over time.

" Our mission is to inspire the innovator in everyone."
